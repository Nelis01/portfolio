Brown v. Board of Education case

The client asked me to write a detailed analysis of the landmark Supreme Court case Brown v. Board of Education (1954), which declared racial segregation in public schools unconstitutional. The document explores the historical context of the case, its impact on the civil rights movement, and its long-term effects on education and racial equality in the United States.
